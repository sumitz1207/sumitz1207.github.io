Summary: Latitude vs. X

For this project, we analyzed the different weather phenomenon according to the latitude of different world cities. We pulled data from OpenWeatherMap API and assembled a dataset for 500 cities.

With Matplotlib, we used the city weather data to graph a variety of plots according to each city's latitude. These weather patterns include temperature, cloudiness, wind speed, and humidity. This site displays the OpenWeatherMap city weather data and the weather maps derived from that data. Also included are explanations of any noticable trends and correlations of city weather and latitude.
